Our History

For over three decades, Chowking has been a household name, serving up a delicious blend of Chinese and Filipino flavors. Our journey began with a humble vision: to create a place where families and friends could come together over hearty, affordable, and mouthwatering meals.

Our story is one of evolution and celebration. From our early beginnings as a simple store in Makati, we’ve now grown into a beloved brand with a nationwide presence.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision: To be the most-loved Chinese Brand QSR in the world.

Mission: To spread the joy of eating delicious Chinese food to everyone.

Our People

Chowking's employees are the heart and soul of the brand, contributing to its success through their dedication, hard work, and passion for serving delicious Chinese-Filipino cuisine. These remarkable individuals come from diverse backgrounds and roles within the company, each playing a crucial part in creating memorable dining experiences for customers.
